If the seller declared the wrong or incorrect dimension of packaging, then the Seller may get a payment discrepancy. To avoid this, the seller should input the correct gross dimension (includes the outer packaging dimension) of packaging to avoid a miscalculated shipping fee.
The system will compute the volumetric weight and will set the amount of shipping fee based on the estimated weight for its own packaging.
  • If the volumetric weight declared is lighter than the actual weight it may result in the deduction of payout to Seller and the Seller Balance will be adjusted.
  • If the volumetric weight declared is heavier than the actual weight, then it may result in the wrong payment of the shipping fee for the buyers.
